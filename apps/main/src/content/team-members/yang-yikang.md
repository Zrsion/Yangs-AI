## Profile

I am currently a Ph.D student at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. 
My research explores the emerging paradigm of Artificial Intelligence Designed Artificial Intelligence (AIDAI), an approach dedicated to leveraging AI techniques to autonomously design AI systems.

I also work on graph generation, with a particular focus on graph diffusion models, exploring how they can generate realistic and diverse graph structures for various applications.

## Research Interests

- Artificial Intelligence Designed Artificial Intelligence
- Graph Diffusion Models
